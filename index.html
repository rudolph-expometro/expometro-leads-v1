Who am I?

My name is Rudolph. In 2018, I launched ExpoMetro, a global public art movement created to make artists visible where life really happens: in the heart of the city.

Since then, more than 6,000 artists from over 100 countries have exhibited in some of the world’s most iconic public spaces, including Paris, London, Madrid and Rome.

I truly believe our cities can become the world’s greatest art galleries. And we’re only getting started.

Rudolph van ValkenburgFounder of ExpoMetro
